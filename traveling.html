Graduation Traveling Plan

After graduating this May, I am excited to travel with my friends to three incredible places: Japan, Taiwan, and Bali. Each place has something unique to offer, from rich cultural experiences to breathtaking landscapes and amazing food.
In Japan, we plan to explore the Tokyo, experiencing its futuristic skyline, shopping districts, and delicious food and night markets. We’ll visit Kyoto for its traditional atmosphere, temples and cherry blossom gardens, and Osaka for its famous street food and bustling nightlife. A day trip to Nara to see the friendly deer in Nara Park is also on our list.
Next, we’ll head to Taiwan, where we’re excited to explore the night markets of Taipei, try new drinks and food, and visit Jiufen, the charming mountain town that inspired the setting of Spirited Away. We also plan to relax in Beitou’s hot springs and take in the stunning views at Taroko Gorge.
Finally, we’ll end our grad in Bali, Indonesia. We plan to visit the Tegallalang Rice Terraces, explore Uluwatu Temple, and relax on the beaches of Seminyak. A sunrise hike to Mount Batur is also on our bucket list.


Japan
  1. Tokyo (Shibuya, Akihabara, Shinjuku)
  2. Kyoto (Fushimi Inari Shrine, Kinkaku-ji)
  3. Osaka (Dotonbori, Universal Studios Japan)
  4. Nara Park
